About

Autumn Von Plinsky is an artist, graphic designer, muralist, etc. currently based out of Brooklyn, NY, and hailing originally from Augusta, GA. Having grown up surrounded by the natural world, most of her work focuses on or draws inspiration from the minutia of nature - the little details that are sometimes overlooked.
